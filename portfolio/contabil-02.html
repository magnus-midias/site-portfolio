The challenge

An award-winning Dublin accountancy firm with 80 years of history that needed a modern digital presence

Merry Mullen is an independent, partner-led firm of chartered accountants and tax advisors based in Dublin 2, with roots going back to 1945. Named Medium Practice of the Year at the Irish Accountancy Awards 2025, the firm serves Irish businesses, family enterprises, and international investors through every stage of their journey — from start-up through growth, succession, and exit.

The challenge was building a website that reflected eight decades of expertise and credibility while feeling modern and accessible, clearly communicating the firm's range of services and its distinctive partner-led, personal approach.

What was delivered

A professional institutional site built around trust, expertise, and client relationships

We developed a fully responsive website with clear service architecture across audit, tax, business advisory, payroll, and international tax. The site features team profiles, a client testimonials section, membership and awards display, and a news section to demonstrate ongoing thought leadership in the Irish accountancy space.

The project included technical SEO optimisation for Dublin-based professional services searches, structured data for the firm's services, and a design system that balances authority with approachability — reflecting the firm's ethos of deep expertise delivered with personal attention.
