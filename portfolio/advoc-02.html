The challenge

An award-winning boutique law firm in the heart of London needed a digital presence as distinctive as its identity

Clintons is an award-winning boutique law firm based in St. Giles, London's West End. It is a market leader in the entertainment, digital media, and creative industries, with strong practices across family, litigation, property, corporate, music, sport, theatre, and immigration law. With a bold visual identity — orange, strong typography, and custom illustrations of its principals — the firm needed a website that reflected this unique personality.

The challenge was creating a digital presence that communicated both legal expertise and Clintons' creative, accessible character — differentiating it from traditional law firms and converting visitors into consultations.

What was delivered

An institutional site with a bold visual identity, practice area presentation, and full team profiles

We developed a full website featuring a slideshow hero, a "What We Do" section with all practice areas in accordion format, a team gallery with custom illustrated principal portraits, a contact page with an illustrated map, and a careers section. The orange and black identity conveys the firm's creative DNA without compromising legal authority.

The project included a mobile-optimised responsive structure, Google Maps integration, content architecture for specialist legal searches in London, and a design system that balances visual boldness with institutional credibility.
